# Health and Safety Policy

## Infection Control
City General Hospital follows strict infection control protocols in line with national guidelines. Alcohol-based hand rub dispensers are placed at all entry points, corridors, and patient care areas. All staff, patients, and visitors are expected to perform hand hygiene upon entering and leaving patient areas.

## Masks and PPE
Masks are recommended in general areas of the hospital. Masks are mandatory in the ICU, oncology ward, isolation rooms, and the dialysis unit. N95 or equivalent masks are required for visitors to immunocompromised patients.

## Isolation Rooms
Patients with suspected or confirmed communicable diseases are managed in designated isolation rooms with negative pressure where appropriate. Visitors to isolation rooms must wear full PPE provided by the nursing team.

## COVID-19 Policy
Patients and visitors who have tested positive for COVID-19 within the past 10 days should not enter the hospital unless for emergency care. A separate COVID-19 screening area is available at Gate 1 for symptomatic walk-in patients. If you are unsure, call before coming in.

## Fire Safety
In case of fire, activate the nearest fire alarm pull station and exit via clearly marked stairwells. Do not use lifts during a fire emergency. Assembly points are located in the parking area (Gate 2) and the garden outside the outpatient building.

## Child Safety
All pediatric wards are access-controlled. Parents must present their ID wristband to enter. Unaccompanied visitors are not permitted in pediatric areas. Please report any suspicious behaviour to the security desk.

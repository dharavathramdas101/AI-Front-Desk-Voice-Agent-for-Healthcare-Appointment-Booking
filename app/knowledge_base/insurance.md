# Insurance and Billing

## Accepted Insurance Plans
City General Hospital accepts the following insurance providers:
- Star Health Insurance
- HDFC ERGO Health Insurance
- Niva Bupa (formerly Max Bupa)
- Care Health Insurance
- New India Assurance
- United India Insurance
- National Insurance Company
- Bajaj Allianz Health Insurance
- Reliance General Insurance (health plans only)
- ICICI Lombard Health Insurance

## Cashless Facility
Cashless treatment is available for all networked insurance plans. Patients must present their insurance card and a valid photo ID at the billing counter before the consultation or procedure. Pre-authorization may be required for planned surgeries and procedures; please check with your insurer at least 48 hours in advance.

## Reimbursement Claims
For non-cashless claims, retain all original bills, discharge summaries, prescriptions, and diagnostic reports. Our billing desk can provide itemized bills and doctor's certificates to support your reimbursement application.

## Self-Pay Patients
Self-pay rates are available at the billing counter. A deposit may be required before elective procedures. The hospital offers an interest-free installment plan for bills exceeding ₹50,000, subject to approval.

# Hospital Departments

## Cardiology
The Cardiology Department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of heart and cardiovascular diseases. Services include ECG, echocardiography, stress testing, cardiac catheterization, pacemaker implantation, and heart failure management. Appointments are available Monday through Saturday.

## Orthopedics
The Orthopedics Department handles conditions affecting bones, joints, ligaments, muscles, and tendons. Specialties include joint replacement (knee, hip), spine surgery, sports medicine, and fracture management. The department has a dedicated physiotherapy unit on the same floor.

## General Medicine
The General Medicine Department provides comprehensive outpatient and inpatient care for adults. Common presentations include fever, infections, hypertension, diabetes, respiratory illness, and preventive health checkups. Walk-in appointments are available on weekdays from 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

## Emergency Department
The Emergency Department (ED) is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. For life-threatening emergencies such as chest pain, stroke symptoms, severe breathing difficulty, or major trauma, patients are triaged immediately upon arrival. The ED contact number is +91-22-4567-8910.

## Radiology and Imaging
Radiology services include X-ray, ultrasound, CT scan, and MRI. A referral from a doctor is required for all imaging. Results are typically ready within 4 to 24 hours depending on the type of scan. The department is located on the ground floor, Wing B.

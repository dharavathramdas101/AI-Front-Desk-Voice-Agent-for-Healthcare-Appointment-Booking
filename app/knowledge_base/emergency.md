# Emergency Services and Contact Numbers

## Emergency Department
The Emergency Department is open 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. A senior emergency physician and nursing team are on duty at all times. For life-threatening conditions — chest pain, difficulty breathing, stroke, major trauma, severe allergic reaction, or loss of consciousness — go directly to the Emergency Department or call the emergency line.

## Emergency Contact
Main Emergency Line: +91-22-4567-8910 (24/7)
Ambulance: +91-22-4567-8911 (24/7)

## What to Bring in an Emergency
When possible, bring: a valid photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or recent test reports. However, treatment will never be withheld for lack of documentation.

## Triage Process
Patients arriving at the ED are triaged immediately using a 5-level priority system. Life-threatening cases are seen instantly. Non-urgent walk-in cases may wait 1–3 hours depending on volume.

## After-Hours for Non-Emergency Issues
For non-emergency concerns outside clinic hours — medication queries, test result questions, or follow-up questions — call the nursing helpline at +91-22-4567-8920. This line is open from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.

## Poison Control
In case of accidental poisoning or overdose, call Poison Control: 1800-116-117 (toll-free, 24/7) and proceed to the nearest Emergency Department.

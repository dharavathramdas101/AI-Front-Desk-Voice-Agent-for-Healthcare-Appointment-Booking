# Visiting Hours

## General Wards
Visitors are welcome Monday through Sunday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM and again from 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM. Each patient may have up to two visitors at a time.

## ICU and Critical Care
ICU visiting hours are restricted to 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM to 6:00 PM. Only immediate family members (spouse, parents, adult children, or siblings) are permitted. Children under the age of 12 are not allowed in the ICU.

## Maternity Ward
New mothers and newborns can be visited from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. The father or one designated support person may stay overnight.

## After-Hours Visits
For emergencies or critical situations outside visiting hours, please speak with the duty nurse at the main nursing station. Exceptions are made at the discretion of the attending physician.

## COVID and Infection Control
All visitors must wash hands or use hand sanitizer upon entry. Visitors who are unwell, have fever, or have been exposed to a communicable disease in the past 7 days are asked to postpone their visit. Masks are recommended but not mandatory in general wards; they are required in the ICU and isolation rooms.

# Doctors at City General Hospital

## Dr. Meera Sharma — Cardiology
Dr. Meera Sharma is an interventional cardiologist with 15 years of experience. She specializes in heart failure, coronary artery disease, angioplasty, and stent placement. She holds an MD from AIIMS Delhi and a fellowship in interventional cardiology from the Cleveland Clinic. Consultation hours: Monday, Wednesday, Friday — 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM.

## Dr. Arjun Nair — Cardiology
Dr. Arjun Nair is an electrophysiologist specializing in arrhythmia, atrial fibrillation, and cardiac ablation. He completed his training at the National Heart Centre Singapore. He performs cardiac device implantations including pacemakers and ICDs. Consultation hours: Tuesday, Thursday — 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM.

## Dr. Priya Desai — Orthopedics
Dr. Priya Desai specializes in joint replacement surgery with a focus on knee and hip arthroplasty. She is also trained in sports medicine and ligament reconstruction. She completed her orthopedic surgery residency at KEM Hospital Mumbai. Consultation hours: Monday, Thursday, Saturday — 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

## Dr. Rahul Mehta — Orthopedics
Dr. Rahul Mehta is a spine surgeon specializing in minimally invasive techniques for disc herniation, spondylosis, and spinal deformities. He has performed over 2,000 spinal procedures. Consultation hours: Tuesday, Friday — 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM.

## Dr. Sunita Iyer — General Medicine
Dr. Sunita Iyer is a general physician with expertise in preventive care, diabetes management, hypertension, thyroid disorders, and chronic disease management. She emphasizes lifestyle-based interventions alongside medication. Consultation hours: Monday through Friday — 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Walk-ins accepted.

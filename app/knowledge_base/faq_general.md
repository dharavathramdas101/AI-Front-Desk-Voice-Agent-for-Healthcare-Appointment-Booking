# Frequently Asked Questions

## How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment through this voice assistant, through our website at citygeneralhospital.in, or by calling the appointment desk at +91-22-4567-8900 between 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM, Monday through Saturday.

## Can I choose my doctor?
Yes, you can request a specific doctor when booking. Subject to availability, we will do our best to accommodate your preference. If your preferred doctor is unavailable, we can offer an appointment with a doctor in the same department.

## How long in advance do I need to book?
Most outpatient appointments are available within 2 to 5 working days. For specialist consultations, we recommend booking at least 3–5 days in advance. Urgent cases are triaged by the department and may be seen sooner.

## Can I reschedule or cancel my appointment?
Yes. You can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your appointment time at no charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a ₹200 administrative fee. Call +91-22-4567-8900 or use this voice assistant to make changes.

## How long will I wait to see the doctor?
Average waiting time for scheduled appointments is 15–30 minutes. Delays may occur if the doctor is attending to an emergency. You will be notified if there is a significant delay.

## What should I bring to my appointment?
Please bring: a valid photo ID, your insurance card, any previous medical records related to your current complaint, a list of your current medications, and any recent lab or imaging reports.

## Do you offer second opinions?
Yes. Any specialist at City General Hospital can provide a second opinion consultation. To arrange this, please bring all relevant prior medical records, imaging, biopsy reports, and previous treatment history.

## Is there a pharmacy on site?
Yes, the hospital pharmacy is located on the ground floor, Wing A, near the main entrance. It is open from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily and stocks most prescription and over-the-counter medications.

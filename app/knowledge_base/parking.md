# Parking and Transportation

## Parking Facility
City General Hospital has a multi-level parking facility on the premises accessible from Main Road Gate 2. The facility operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Charges are ₹50 for the first two hours and ₹30 per hour thereafter. A daily cap of ₹200 applies.

## Disabled and Accessible Parking
Designated disabled parking bays are located on the ground floor of the parking facility, near the main entrance lift. A valid disability certificate or PwD sticker must be displayed. These bays are free of charge.

## Drop-Off and Pick-Up
A drop-off/pick-up zone is available at the main hospital entrance on the ground level. Vehicles may not park at the entrance for more than 10 minutes.

## Valet Parking
Valet parking is available at the main entrance from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM. The charge is ₹150 flat.

## Public Transportation
The hospital is located 500 metres from Metro Station (Blue Line, City Centre stop). Bus routes 45, 67, and 103 stop at the hospital gate. Auto-rickshaws and cabs can be hailed from the hospital exit at Gate 1.

## Ambulance and Emergency Drop-Off
Ambulances and emergency vehicles should use Gate 3 on the eastern side of the campus, which leads directly to the Emergency Department. This gate is staffed 24 hours.
